# Project Brief: Fun Lovin Tattoo - The Modern Archivist ## 1. Project Overview **Fun Lovin Tattoo** is a professional tattoo brand established in 2007. The goal is to build a high-end, multi-page digital presence that balances its boutique studio roots with its premium mobile tattoo services for weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations. ## 2. Creative Direction: "The Modern Archivist" The brand identity has evolved from a clean "Ink & Ivory" style to a bolder, more creative "Ink & Archive" aesthetic. * **Visual Tone:** Moody, editorial, and professional yet "grungy" and creative. * **Color Palette:** Deep blacks, ivory/cream, and high-contrast white. * **Typography:** Elegant, high-contrast serifs (Newsreader) paired with functional, modern sans-serifs. * **Design Elements:** Ink-bleed organic shapes, custom distressed borders, layered magazine-style layouts, and a "archival" feel (e.g., volume numbers, grid coordinates). ## 3. Core User Flows & Sitemap 1. **Homepage:** A high-impact entry point showcasing the dual nature of the business (Mobile vs. Studio). 2. **Mobile Services ("Ink on the Move"):** Dedicated page for event bookings, featuring: * Weddings, Birthdays, and Pop-up experiences. * Safety and professionalism standards. * Tiered event packages. 3. **Studio Gallery & Location ("The Sanctuary"):** * Curated portfolio ("Permanent Collections"). * Artist spotlight (e.g., Marco V., Chief Archivist). * Studio coordinates, hours, and map. 4. **Pricing & Event Menu ("The Editorial Index"):** * Transparent "from" pricing for major service categories. * Modular add-ons (Custom flash, additional artists, overtime). * Booking protocols and technical/regulatory standards. ## 4. Key Functional Requirements * **Transparent Pricing:** Clear "from" prices and modular service blocks. * **Inquiry-Driven UX:** Prominent "Book Now" or "Inquire" calls to action across all pages. * **Visual Portfolio:** High-quality imagery focusing on "The Hand Behind the Archive" and the final "relics" (tattoos). * **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both Desktop and Mobile devices. ## 5.
