# Your Comfort is Priority

Tattooing is a physical and mental experience. We want you to feel empowered and relaxed throughout the entire process.

## 1. Snacks and Water
- **Stay Hydrated**: Bring water to sip during the session to help your body manage pain and adrenaline.
- **Sugary Snacks**: Keep your blood sugar up. Fruit, chocolate, or a granola bar can provide a vital boost if you start to feel lightheaded.

## 2. Communication is Key
- **Breaks**: Do not be afraid to ask for a break. If you need to stretch, adjust your chair, or use the restroom, just speak up.
- **Honesty**: We want you to be 100% happy with the **stencil placement**. If it's slightly off or you want it shifted, tell us! It’s much easier to re-apply the stencil than to live with a placement you're not happy with.
- **How You Feel**: Be honest about how you’re feeling. If you start to feel faint or the pain is becoming unmanageable, we can pause and regroup.

## 3. Numbing Options
We offer numbing cream for certain sessions. If you're considering this, please **discuss it with us before your appointment**. Some creams can change skin texture and affect how it takes ink, so we want to ensure we use the best option for your specific piece.

***

Remember: You are the most important part of this session. We're here to make sure you have the best experience possible.
