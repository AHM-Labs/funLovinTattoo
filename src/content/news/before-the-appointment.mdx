# Pre-Appointment Preparation

A great tattoo starts with good skin and a prepared body. Follow these steps to ensure you're ready for your session.

## 1. The 15-Minute Rule
Please arrive **15 minutes early**. This gives us time to settle your bags, get you a drink, and handle any final paperwork or stencil adjustments without rushing your session.

## 2. Skin Preparation
- **Cleaning**: Arrive with clean, healthy skin. Avoid spray tans or heavy perfumes on the day.
- **Moisturizing**: Start moisturizing the area daily for **one week before** your appointment. This keeps the skin supple and ready for ink. *Do not moisturize on the morning of your session.*
- **Shaving**: We generally recommend letting us handle the shaving to avoid irritation or nicks. If you must shave, use a new razor and be extremely careful.

## 3. Fuel Your Body
- **Eat a Big Meal**: Never come to a tattoo appointment on an empty stomach. Your body needs blood sugar to manage the adrenaline.
- **Hydrate**: Drink plenty of water in the 48 hours leading up to your session.
- **Rest**: Get a full night’s sleep. Your body handles pain much better when rested.

***

> **Important**: No alcohol for at least 24 hours before your session. It thins the blood and will affect the quality of your tattoo.
